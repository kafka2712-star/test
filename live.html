About Gold Pricing

How It Works

Our system uses CORS proxies to fetch raw HTML from public gold price websites like Kitco, GoldPrice.org, and LBMA. We then parse the HTML using regex patterns to extract current prices. No APIs or tokens required!

Market Hours

Gold markets trade nearly 24 hours a day, Sunday evening through Friday afternoon (New York time). Most liquidity occurs during London and New York trading sessions.
